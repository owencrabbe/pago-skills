# How to install and use a PAGO skill

These skills are plain text files. You do not need to code, and you do not need an account.

## Option 1: Paste it in (works with any AI assistant)

1. Open the skill folder you want, for example `skills/lease-review`.
2. Open `SKILL.md` and copy everything in it.
3. Start a new chat with your AI assistant.
4. Paste the skill contents as your first message, then on the next line write what you need. Example: "Here is the skill you will use. Then review the lease I am about to paste."
5. Send. The assistant now follows the skill.

## Option 2: Save it as a skill (Claude and agents that support skill files)

1. Download the `SKILL.md` file, or the whole skill folder.
2. Add it to your agent's skills folder or upload it where your agent accepts skills.
3. The agent will load it automatically when your request matches what the skill does.

## Tips

- Give the skill real material to work with. A lease, a list of cities, your actual emails. The more specific you are, the better the result.
- You can edit any skill to fit your situation. They are yours to change.
- These tools give you information and drafts. For legal, financial, or tax decisions, confirm with a licensed professional before you act.
